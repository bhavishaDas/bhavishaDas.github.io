Human Skills

I would like to gain public speaking skills. I get a bit flustered around larger groups of people especially if I don't know them. This skill is important to me because regardless of wherever I end up this skill will remain with me forever. It will also help me in my role as a software developer when it comes to explaining my tech designs to my team and allow me to be heard.

Strengths and Limitations

My strengths are that I have the ability to understand complex problems once I take a step back. I also have a good work ethic and my previous role as a Travel Consultant has conditioned me well for multimasking. Being in the sales industry, I have good communication skills and have extensive experience in managing stakeholder/client expectations. I tend to get flustered and panicked when I am confronted with a problem which is hard to solve. I am aware that as a software developer, I will come across a zillion problems I won’t have an answer to. This course is actually helping me realise that if I take a step back, things fall into pieces. I also lack confidence - especially while I am doing this course. Self doubt creeps in from time to time which is a bummer.

I’m aware I can get flustered when I get stuck on a problem and I intend to mitigate it by taking more short breaks and coming back to the problem. I’ve tired this previously and it seems to be working for me

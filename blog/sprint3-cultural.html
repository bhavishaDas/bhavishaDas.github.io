Emotional Intelligence


What is Emotional intelligence?

EQ is having social skills, empathy and self-awareness. It’s the ability to understand and manage your own emotions and the emotions of the people around you.


How is it different to IQ?

EQ stands for emotional quotient, and it refers to one's emotions whereas IQ is measuring one’s academic intelligence by a test. EQ and IQ are the difference between being able to function well and being able to function well enough to succeed.


Why is it important?

Having a high IQ is an advantage but if you don't have any EQ you won't be able to relate to others and wont have the ability to empathise. Having an EQ can make all the difference to your future career as nobody would want to work alongside somebody that comes across as self centered or can’t control their emotions.

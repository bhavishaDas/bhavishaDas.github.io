Neuroplasticity


What is Neuroplasticity?

Neuroplasticity is the ability to rewire our brain. Our brain changes throughout our lives and it allows us to adapt, learn and even recover. When we learn the brain forms new pathways. Each new lesson has the potential to connect new neurons and change our brain’s way of thinking.
Understanding the principles of Neuroplasticity is beneficial for people from different walks of life. Someone who might have suffered from a brain stroke might recover using Neuroplasticity. Another benefit of practicing Neuroplasticity is that it enhances the learning capabilities of individuals.
Some ways to increase your neuroplasticity is to keep learning and practising as it strengthens the brain. Another important element would be to get enough sleep as your brain needs to reset failing to do this impairs your memory.
Neuroplasticity will be beneficial for me because I’m attempting to learn something new.


What is Growth Mindset?

Growth mindset is being able to change the way one thinks. Glass half full rather than glass half empty vibe. People with growth mindset tend to believe that with hard work and practice they can improve their skills. Developing a growth mindset helps in keeping a positive attitude.

Identity, Values and Strengths

Core Personal Values

One of my core values is work ethic and being honest to the work I undertake. I really don't appriciate when people don't follow up with their commitments.
When the lockdown started, we in the team knew that we will be made redundant soon. Before that though, we had a heap of bookings which were supposed to be cancelled. Being in sales, cancelled bookings meant losing on a heap of money along with the redundancy looming.
I had a to make a decision. It was either to sit at home and enjoy the wage subsidy knowing that I was close to loosing my job OR front up the harsh reality and get the work done
I chose the later and it felt right. Didn't make a change in my paycheck but I felt I had done the right thing.
I feel that this comes from growing up in a household where I have seen everyone hustle. I feel greatful to have grown up in an atomosphere where hardwork is recognised.

Strengths and Limitations.

My strengths are that I have the ability to understand complex problems once I take a step back. I also have a good work ethic and my previous role as a Travel Consultant has conditioned me well for multimasking. Being in the sales industry, I have good communication skills and have extensive experience in managing stakeholder/client expectations.
I tend to get flustered and panicked when I am confronted with a problem which is hard to solve. Back in my previous role, I was working in a tight knit team and eveything was going great. One of my teammates got promoted to a team lead role. We all were super happy for them! With time, they started micro-managing everyone. All bookings needed their approval (which was never the case with the previous TL.) Five people in the team quit, it wasn't a good time. I had a 1:1 with them and highlighted what the issue was - they did acknowledge the problem which was refreshing. We started having regular catch-ups and they asked for constructive feedback.
Eventually things went back to normal which was great.
If I was to do something differently, I would like to step in early and give them feedback and not wait until the problem became worse.
